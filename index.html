Akwa Ibom state

Akwa Ibom State is a state in the South-South geopolitical zone of Nigeria. It borders Cross River State to the east, Rivers State and Abia State to the west and northwest, and to the south by the Atlantic Ocean. Akwa Ibom is blessed with many natural and human resources, economically, Akwa Ibom State is based around the production of crude oil and natural gas as the highest oil-producing state in the country. Key minor industries involve agriculture as the state has substantial cocoyam, yam, and plantain crops along with fishing.
